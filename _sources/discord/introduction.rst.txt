-------------------------
‘THE BOYZ’ Discord server
-------------------------

‘THE BOYZ’ was formed in attempt to gather people from Fundy's offline chat
to meet better outside the Twitch chat and have fun in informal atmosphere,
with features like voice channels and bots.

While Fundy has an official server, it is crowded by many people, including
those who are not watching Twitch and do not participate in chat, or offline
chat specifically.

Our server is invite-only, mostly people who are active in offline chat and
do not have conflicting behaviour invited quickly. Although, invites can also
be requested by the members of the server.
